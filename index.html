What is InterviewEQ?

⋮The Problem
ⓧThe Problem

InterviewEQ is for developers who want to build their soft skills to get jobs. Qualified coders can still fail to connect with the interviewer. InterviewEQ exists to help solve that problem.

⋮The Solution
ⓧThe Solution

InterviewEQ helps judge the emotional tone of recorded speech. By better understanding your tone, you can relate to interviewers better. By relating to interviewers better, you can get better jobs.

⋮How It Works
ⓧHow It Works

Record the audio of your mock interview below. The app uses speech-to-text conversion to transcribe the audio. The text is fed into an emotion analysis engine that then displays the prominent emotions present in your recording.

Let's Get Started

Click Start to begin recording. Click Reset to stop without submitting. Click Submit to stop and submit for results.

Browser & Device Requirements

At this time InterviewEQ works exclusively in Google Chrome on PCs and Android mobile devices.
